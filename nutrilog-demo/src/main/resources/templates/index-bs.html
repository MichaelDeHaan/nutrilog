About NutriLog: Smart Nutrition

Nutrilog is a food tracker with an emphasis on nutritional education and safety. With NutriLog, you can see the nutritional values of the foods you consume, giving you an enhanced awareness of your overall nutritional status. With NutriLog, you can customize your settings to flag ingredients you may be allergic to, such as eggs, peanuts, and shellfish. We also have nutrition specialists who can create customized nutrition plans to aid you on your path to optimal nutrition.
