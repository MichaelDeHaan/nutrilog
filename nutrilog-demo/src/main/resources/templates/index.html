About NutriLog: Smart Nutrition

Nutrilog: Smart Nutrition is the lastest food tracker and logger that will inform you about the nutritional values of the foods that you consume, leading to overall better health outcomes. In addition, Nutrilog can provide information about the ingredients that are in foods that are common allergens such as eggs, peanuts, shellfish, etc. so that users with allergies know what foods to avoid. But it doesn't just provide allergen information, it also recommends allergen free alternatives! We also have Nutrition Specialists that can assist users with finding healthy foods and make custom nutrition plans made specifically for individual users.
